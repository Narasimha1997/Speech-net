Speech.net

Speech.net is a online website where you can convert your document-text to speech for free, You don't have to worry about the size of document, just upload and wait for the process to take place, You will get .mp3 file of the resultant-speech.

Active on gitHub

Speech.net is actively developed on gitHub, If you have any new ideas fell free to contribute, You can also join the community of developers to plan something big
